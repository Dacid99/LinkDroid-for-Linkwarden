<b>LinkDroid is an companion app for <em>Linkwarden</em> on Android.</b>
You can quickly share links directly to your <em>Linkwarden</em> instance via the context menu, access the webapp and manage your collection!
Various options help in improving your <em>Linkwarden</em> experience on Android!

<ul>
    <li>Quick link sharing</li>
    <li>Built-in web browsing</li>
    <li>Direct connection to your Linkwarden instance</li>
    <li>Tablet support</li>
    <li>Support for various languages including fr, es, de, pt, pl, zh, uk, ...</li>
    <li>No tracking or third party-services</li>
    <li>Completely FOSS under GPLv3 license</li>
</ul>

The app works best on Android version 13 and above. On lower versions the colors of the webview are messed up.
If you know a fix for that please let me know!
Make sure to add an auth token in the settings for an optimal experience.

You're always welcome to leave questions, feedback and issues in the repositories on gitlab and github.
We're always looking for new translators and contributors! If you have an idea for the app let us know!

Support of API levels 21 to 26 was dropped in version 2.0.0 in favour of modern features.
